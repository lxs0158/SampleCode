OUR PRODUCTS

Media Monitoring Service

Web-based media monitoring service to measure your marketing effectiveness and protect your reputation in the news media.

Main Features

News Tracking Systerm

Track relevant news from desired media list, analyze data based on searching keyword and generate various media coverage reports instantly.

Media Database

Media database with Web-based user interface. Clients can easily view the archived news, adjust search options and generate report by log on to the website.

Newsletter and Distribution

Built-in newsletter and distribute function to release news or announcement to clients' email contact list and specific accounts in social media.

Multiple User Access

Multiple users can access the application simultaneously with different privileges. Users can maintain and update their media list without any knowledge of databases.

E-Procurement System

Cloud-based mobile friendly e-procurement system for better and easier procurement management for government and other public agency.

Main Features

Vendor registration

Self-service vendor registration with hard stop requirements, commodity and Service category code identification, vendor notification and directory search.

Solicitation process

Upload solicitation documents in various formats, automated vendor notification and event management, secured e-Bidding and administrative support.

Evaluation process

Event set up with panel members registration, automated evaluation functions including sharing documents, criteria development, and automatic email notifications.

Contract Management process

Upload and edit contract documents, view contract history, track contract modifications and milestones, generate reports, subcontractor and supplier performance management.

Insurance and Bond Management

Add/edit insurance certificates, view existing and past ACORD certificates for history, run reports, automated and custom notification settings.

Emergency Services Operations

Emergency suppliers list with additional categorization, complete vendor form integrated with registration, automatic notification, reports by pre-defined categories.

Standard Reporting capabilities

Solicitation and project outreach reports, activity and management reports, vendor reports and searches, configurable inventory and operations reporting.

Sales Signal Processing

Demand Discovery, Competition Analysis, Sales Channel Recommendation, Missed Opportunities Uncover.

Linkedin Information Retrieval

Linkedin Information Retrieval and Data Mining software development for Business Intelligence, Sales Lead Extraction, Market Trend Analysis and Competitor Analysis.
