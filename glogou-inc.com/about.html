ABOUT US

Our Company

Our company is an experienced software development company headquartered in Silicon Valley, CA. Our team includes experienced Silicon Valley software consultants and business people. Many of our founding team members are Stanford Ph.Ds and MBAs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to use our expertise to provide high quality custom software development services at very competitive prices for our clients. We can do either large scale or small size software projects. Listening to customers is our top priority. No project is too small for us.

Our Technology

We use future proof technologies to ensure that our customers will get the best solution. Technologies we use include Hadoop for big data, HTML5/CSS3, Javascript and CMS for frontend developments, and Python and Django framework for backend developments .
